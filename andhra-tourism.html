Popular Destinations

The world's richest temple atop the sacred Seshachalam hills, drawing millions of devotees annually to seek blessings at the Sri Venkateswara Swamy Temple.

Nestled in the Eastern Ghats, this picturesque valley offers coffee plantations, waterfalls, and the famous Borra Caves carved over millions of years.

Gandikota

Kadapa District Best: Oct-Feb

Unforgettable Experiences

Every corner of Andhra Pradesh tells a unique story waiting to be explored

⛪

Spiritual Trails

Walk the sacred steps of Tirumala, visit the Pancharama Kshetras, and experience the divine energy of ancient temples dating back centuries.

🌊

Coastal Escapes

Relax on golden sands at RK Beach, Suryalanka, Manginapudi, and Bheemunipatnam along Andhra's 974 km pristine coastline.

⛰

Hill Retreats

Trek through the lush Eastern Ghats at Araku Valley, Lambasingi (Kashmir of Andhra), and Horsley Hills for cool mountain breezes.

🌉

Heritage Walks

Explore the ancient stupas of Amaravati, the intricate carvings of Lepakshi, and the magnificent forts of Chandragiri and Kondapalli.

🎣

Cultural Festivals

Witness the grandeur of Brahmotsavam at Tirumala, the vibrant dance of Kuchipudi, and the folk celebrations of Sankranti across the state.

🍲

Andhra Cuisine

Savor the world-famous spicy Gongura pickle, Hyderabadi-style Biryani, Pesarattu dosas, and the unforgettable Rayalaseema Ragi Sankati.

Rich Heritage

Culture & Traditions

Kuchipudi Dance

Born in the village of Kuchipudi, this classical dance form blends graceful movements with dramatic storytelling, recognized as one of India's eight major classical dances.

Festivals of Joy

From the spectacular Brahmotsavam drawing millions, to Sankranti celebrations with colorful rangoli and kite flying — Andhra's festivals are a vibrant spectacle.

Ancient Buddhist Heritage

Andhra Pradesh is home to some of India's oldest Buddhist sites. The Amaravati Stupa and the rock-cut caves of Undavalli showcase a rich Buddhist legacy from the 2nd century BCE.

Travel Guide

How to Reach

  • By Air: International airports at Visakhapatnam, Vijayawada (Gannavaram), and Tirupati. Well connected to major Indian cities.
  • By Rail: Extensive railway network with major junctions at Vijayawada, Visakhapatnam, Guntur, and Tirupati.
  • By Road: NH-16 and NH-44 connect the state. APSRTC buses run to every corner of Andhra Pradesh.

Best Time to Visit

  • Winter (Oct-Feb): Ideal for all destinations — pleasant weather, perfect for sightseeing and beach visits.
  • Summer (Mar-Jun): Best for hill stations like Araku Valley and Horsley Hills to escape the heat.
  • Monsoon (Jul-Sep): Lush green landscapes, waterfalls at their peak — perfect for nature lovers.

Travel Tips

  • Book Tirumala darshan tickets online in advance through the TTD portal.
  • Dress modestly when visiting temples — many require traditional attire.
  • Try the local cuisine but ease into the spice levels if you're not accustomed.
  • Carry cash for rural areas and small towns where digital payments may be limited.
  • Hire licensed guides for heritage sites to fully appreciate the history.
